Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Zap. ("we", "us", or "our") is a deal discovery app that aggregates publicly available deals from e-commerce platforms. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate the app:

  • Push notification token: If you allow notifications, we store an anonymous device token (Expo Push Token) to deliver deal alerts. This token is not linked to your identity.
  • Usage data: Anonymous click counts on deals (we track how many times a deal is tapped, not who tapped it)
  • Device information: Basic device type and OS version for crash reporting

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any personally identifiable information. You can opt out of push notifications at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display deal popularity (click counts shown in the app)
  • To improve deal curation and app performance
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues

3. Affiliate Disclosure

Zap. participates in affiliate programmes with Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, and other e-commerce platforms. When you tap "View Offer" and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This does not influence which deals we feature — we only show deals we believe are genuinely good value.

4. Third-Party Links

Deals in the app link to third-party websites (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.). Once you leave our app, their privacy policies apply. We are not responsible for their content or practices.

5. Data Storage

Anonymous click data is stored on secure servers. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

Zap. is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
